Meet Christine: Passionate, Professional, Patient

Welcome to The Pampered Pooch! My name is Christine, and my lifelong love for animals has culminated in this dream salon. With over 10 years of professional grooming experience, I believe in a gentle, fear-free approach. I treat every dog as if they were my own, ensuring a safe, calm, and positive experience from the moment they walk through the door.

My qualifications and commitment to ongoing education mean your pet is in the best hands. I specialize in handling anxious dogs and am dedicated to making grooming a happy and routine part of their lives.
